01

A "request" is any task or change you'd like us to handle, whether it's small adjustments or more extensive work on your custom-designed platform or website. Our subscription plans are designed to give you control over the number of active requests you can have at once. With our standard plans, you can have one active design and/or development request, similar to how streaming services limit the number of devices you can use simultaneously. If you're looking to scale up or need faster turnaround times, you can easily upgrade to a plan with two requests, effectively doubling the amount of work we can do for you.
02

We offer you the flexibility to make design/development request seamlessly through Trello. This can be done in a variety of different ways such as directly through Trello, google docs, wire-frames, existing designs or videos if you do not like writing things out. Basically as long as it is in Trello, it is good.
03

You have the freedom to add as many requests as you'd like to your backlog. These requests will be delivered one at a time. This approach ensures that each task is completed to your satisfaction before moving on to the next one. If you need work done faster or wish to have multiple requests in progress simultaneously, you can easily upgrade your plan to scale.
